Foundation


The foundation of Sun & Kris was laid with Aakriti – a retail outlet in Kirti Nagar - one of Delhi’s largest furniture markets. For years, Aakriti consistently delivered hand-made furniture that stood out for their finish and quality. Aakriti soon became a favourite among customers.

With the intention to provide customers world–class furniture from across the world, Sun & Kris launched three more retail outlets - Indesign, Ambiance and Arterio. Today, Sun & Kris has a wide collection of furniture from around the globe. And is perhaps the biggest name in Delhi’s biggest furniture market.



Made by Hand. Made to Order.


Essentially, there are two things that make a good piece of art. The right tools and a genius mind. A philosophy that validates Aakriti’s existence. However, there is yet another element that we believe defines a masterpiece from the rest. It’s called Inspiration.

At Aakriti, we don’t just design classical furniture, we pay ode to an era gone by. After all, bringing a style of art as old as a century is an art in itself. And that’s possible because we are inspired to make hand-crafted creations. Even when it is custom made.
